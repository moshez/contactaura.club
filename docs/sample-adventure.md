# Sample Adventure: The Lost Tome

This is a sample adventure written in Markdown format to demonstrate MyST support.

## Synopsis

A mysterious ancient tome has been discovered in the ruins beneath the old library.
The party must investigate its origins while avoiding the dangers that lurk in the darkness.

## Background

The **Lost Tome of Eternity** was written centuries ago by the archmage Caldris.
It contains powerful knowledge that many would kill to possess.

### Key Points

- The tome is protected by magical wards
- Multiple factions seek to claim it
- Time is running out before the ruins collapse

## Notable NPCs

### Librarian Meredith

- **Race:** Human
- **Class:** Scholar (non-combatant)
- **Motivation:** Preserve historical knowledge
- **Secret:** Knows more than she admits

### Captain Torvin

- **Race:** Dwarf
- **Class:** Fighter
- **Motivation:** Protect the town
- **Hook:** Lost his brother in the ruins years ago

## Encounters

### 1. The Collapsed Entrance

The main entrance to the old library has collapsed. The party must find an alternate route.

**Skill Checks:**
- DC 12 Investigation to find the hidden servant's entrance
- DC 15 Athletics to clear rubble (risky approach)

### 2. Guardian Constructs

```
Encounter: 3x Stone Guardians
CR: 2 each
Tactics: Defend the inner chambers
Treasure: Ancient coins (50 gp value)
```

## Rewards

Upon completing the adventure:

- [ ] 500 XP per character
- [ ] The Lost Tome (plot item)
- [ ] Favor from Librarian Meredith
- [ ] Optional: +1 Weapon found in armory

## DM Notes

:::{note}
The tome should be a plot hook for future adventures, not a source of immediate power.
:::

:::{warning}
If the party takes too long (more than 2 hours in-game), the ruins begin to collapse!
:::

## Possible Outcomes

1. **Success**: Party retrieves the tome and escapes safely
2. **Partial Success**: Tome retrieved but casualties or complications occur
3. **Failure**: Tome lost, ruins collapse, or tome falls into wrong hands

---

*This adventure is designed for 3-5 characters of level 3-4 and should take approximately 2-3 hours to complete.*
